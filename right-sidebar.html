NC CENTRAL UNIVERISTY BIOMEDICAL LAB:  Intern 

• Grew cell cultures of Microglia, Neuronal and Astrocyte cells from rats. 

• Treated cells with with Nano plastic particles. 

• Performed cell lysis on treated cells. 

• Utilized various scientific machines; inverted microscope, centrifuge, UV treated fume hood.

 • Learned widely used cell culture techniques such as Western Blot method, Coomasie Straining technique, Chemi illumination detection method.

 • Utilized the Coomasie Straining technique to visualize protein ladders in dead cells. 

• Evaluated the extent to which cell death occurred via nano plastics using the Chemi illumination detection method. 

• Write paper about facilitative effects of microplastics on the development of Alzheimer's Disease
